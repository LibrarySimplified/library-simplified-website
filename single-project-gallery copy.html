NYPL Open E-book Hackathon: Reflowing Page Scans for Small Screens

On January 14, the JSTOR Labs team took part in New York Public Library's Open Book Hack Day. The project was an experiment in improving the reading experience of page-scan content on a mobile device. Dubbed ReflowIt and best experienced using a smartphone, the project re-flows page scan content for a handful of articles so that it renders more easily on a small screen, and does so without having to rely on imperfect OCR text.

Date: Jan. 23, 2015
Authors: Ron Snyder, Alex Humphreys
Project Link: ReflowIt
