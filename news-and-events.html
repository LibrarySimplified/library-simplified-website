    Library Simplified at ALA Midwinter Town Hall
    ALA Midwinter | Pennsylvania Convention Center, Room PCC-117

    Library Simplified will participate in a town hall discussion with the leading library industry companies/executive to address a vision for an emerging "Library Content Services Model" fulfilled by cross-industry interoperability among Library Management Services platforms and other ILSs, Content Providers, and Discovery Services.

  • Events Open Book Hack Weekend - at NYPL

    Library Simplified organizes an Open Book Hackathon
    Open Book Hack Weekend | The New York Public Library

    Library Simplified with NYPL Labs, Readium Foundation, O’Reilly Media, Perseus Books, Hypothes.is, Google, and Datalogics for Open Book Hack Weekend at the New York Public Library (at 42nd Street)are hosting a digital book open source and content development event to promote HTML5, EPUB, and the Open Web Platform to imporove the reading expereince. Particpants broaught theirhacking skills, software/content projects and ideas for projects. Through self-organizing teams they pursued creative app/service/content hacks to help us imagine the future of digital books, and advance the open source and open API building blocks needed for the diverse ecosystem of authors, designers, developers, publishers, libraries, booksellers, and readers.

    • 11 January 2014
    • The New York Public Library, 5th Avenue at 42nd Street, New York, NY
