SimplyE for Android

One App, all the books

SimplyE for Android

SimplyE on Android brings the public library to your Android phone. Works with all major library eBook vendors so you only need one app to access the full collection of your public library's collection

  • Compatible with Android ver 4.4 and above
  • Works with all major library eBook vendors
  • Optimized for both smartphone and tablet

Discover

Browse and experience your lirbaries collection like never before.
Explore by genre, subject, audience or language. See best sellers and staff picks or recommendations.

Borrow and Read

Download free ebooks to keep, borrow or reserve popular titles simply and easily with just a library card. No app downloads from differnet vendors, no personal information required, just your library card...nice.

Catalog

Easly toggle between libraries and collections. Browse and search categories of books by subject or genre, audience or language. See similar titles by author or series as well as similar works the library recommends.

My Books

Quickly access books you have already downloaded or borrowed. See days left to read or return early when you are finished.

Reservations

Easily check the status of books you have reserved and boorow when become available or cancel previous reservations.

Settings

Add libraries and collections, manage your membership on a library by library basis. Find and access usefull how to and support resources from your library.
