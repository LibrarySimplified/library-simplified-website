  • Compatible with Android ver 4.4 and above

  • Optimized for both smartphone and tablet

The Platform

The SimplyE Library Ebook App brings the power of open source software and community development to libraries world-wide. The platform, built by libraries for libraries delivers unprecendented control and simplicity to library ebook services.

Web Catalog

The OPDS Web Catalog provides libraries a unified ebook catalog for discovery and online reading.

Mobile Apps

Discover, borrow and read in 3 clicks or less on Android and iOS devices with just your library card. Simple, private and secure.

Cloud Based Infrastructure

SimplyE Circulation Managers create the magic behind SimplyE. Elevate your library ebook service and bring it to the cloud.

Privacy and Security

Privacy, anonymity, security. Your patrons are your business, not your vendors or the webs. SimplyE is built with library values and principles at the core.

Analytics

Get a detailed view of your ebook collection's circulation regardless of vendor without sacrificeing patron privacy.
