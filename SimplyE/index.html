SimplyE The Library Ebook App

Built by Libraries for Libraries

SimplyE is a next generation e-reading app built with open source technologies, designed to simplify finding, borrwoing and reading ebooks from libraries. Free app, free books, what's not to like?

Go to your App Store today to download and begin reading even if you don't have a library card.

The Platform

The SimplyE Library Ebook App brings the power of open source software and community development to libraries world-wide. The platform, built by libraries for libraries delivers unprecendented control and simplicity to library ebook services.

Web Catalog

The OPDS Web Catalog provides libraries a unified ebook catalog for discovery and online reading.

Mobile Apps

Discover, borrow and read in 3 clicks or less on Android and iOS devices with just your library card. Simple, private and secure.

Cloud Based Infrastructure

SimplyE Circulation Managers create the magic behind SimplyE. Elevate your library ebook service and bring it to the cloud.

Privacy and Security

Privacy, anonymity, security. Your patrons are your business, not your vendors or the webs. SimplyE is built with library values and principles at the core.

Analytics

Get a detailed view of your ebook collection's circulation regardless of vendor without sacrificeing patron privacy.

Open Source

Did we mention we are an open source project? Come build and design with us. Help us bring access and knowledge through books to the world.

What Others Say

Don't take our word, hear what librarians from across the country are saying.

“I am very excited that the Connecticut State Library is working in collaboration with NYPL to deploy the Library Simplified App statewide in Connecticut and to be partnering on the development of the LEAP project. This partnership will greatly enhance access to eBooks through Connecticut's public libraries.”

Kendall F. Wiggin, State Librarian, Connecticut.

“SimplyE is allowing Maryland libraries to integrate state-wide consortial and individually owned digital content in one easy-to-use app, branded for each individual library system. It eliminates the need for multiple logins on different platforms and puts our libraries in the foreground as the true provider and funding agent of digital content. We’re thrilled to deploy it!”

Michael Blackwell, Director, St Mary’s County Library.

“SimplyE is a leading example of how libraries are making great use of IMLS grant funds to step-up and step-in to app development that improves library service for all. Rather than building local solutions to suit every snowflake, NYPL and their library and consortial partners across the country have worked together to solve two big problems that complicate library eBooks: multiple apps and patron privacy.”

Paula Mackinon - Director, Califa.
