About Library Simplified

The open source project for the SimplyE platform.

What is Library Simplified

Library Simplified is an open source project with the mission of improving access to ebooks from libraries. Its goals are to simplify access by promoting interoperable and accessible standards, and technolgies as well as their use in the digital publishing and library industry. We beleive that through libraries, book and reading, people gain access to knowledge, information and culture to which they are justily entitled so that they may pursue opportunity and growth.

"The man who acquires the ability to take full possession of his own mind may take possession of anything else to which he is justly entitled."
