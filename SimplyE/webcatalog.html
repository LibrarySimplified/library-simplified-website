SimplyE Web Catalog

One catalog, all your ebook collections

SimplyE Web Catalog is fast, responsive and works cross browser to bring all your ebook collections into one inutive and easy to use E-Book central site. No more vendor silos.

  • Works with all major library eBook vendors
  • With all desktop and mobile web browsers
  • Can be customized to reflect library or consortia's style and brand

Borrow and Read

The SimplyE Platforms Web Catalog is an Open Publications Distribution System (OPDS) client web application which allows you to deploy a simple unified ebook catalog on your library's website. It is the same union catalog on the SimplyE mobile app uses. This allows you to create a dedicated, browse and searchable catalog of all you ebooks.

Bennefits

Link out to a single unified view of your vendor hosted books. Users see and borrow from your entire ebook collection not just one vendor's books. You can also apply your own style sheet that it reflects your library's online look and feel.

Borrow, Download and Read

Quickly access books you have already downloaded or borrowed. See days left to read or return early when you are finished. Open non-DRM EPUBS and read in the browser or open your distributors streaming reader.

Integrate with your ILS

Use MARC 856 "Electronic Digital Resource Record" to link OPDS Web Catalog Book bibpage urls to your ILS catalog or to open the SimplyE app to that same book.
