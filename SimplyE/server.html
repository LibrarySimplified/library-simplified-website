SimplyE Cloud Applications

Circulation, Metadata and Content Servers

Circulation Manager

The SimplyE Circulation Manager is the heart of the SimplyE platform. The application provides an agregated e-book shelf, union catalog, and service gateway to all you ebook service provider APIs. It is built as a high performance, web scalable application that can be run on clud or enterise infrastructure with minimal costs

Bennefits

Link out to a single unified view of your vendor hosted books. Users see and borrow from your entire ebook collection not just one vendor's books. You can also apply your own style sheet that it reflects your library's online look and feel.

Borrow, Download and Read

Quickly access books you have already downloaded or borrowed. See days left to read or return early when you are finished. Open non-DRM EPUBS and read in the browser or open your distributors streaming reader.

Integrate with your ILS

Use MARC 856 "Electronic Digital Resource Record" to link OPDS Web Catalog Book bibpage urls to your ILS catalog or to open the SimplyE app to that same book.
